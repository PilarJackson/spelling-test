How It Works

Each level/sub-level has two parts: a Review Page and a Test Page.

  1. Review Page: Click the sub-level link to view all the words and see the image.
  2. Test Page: When ready, click 'Start Test' on the test page. You'll be presented one word at a time. Type what you hear and press Enter or click 'Submit Answer'. You must get all words correct to pass the sub-level.

Your progress is saved automatically once you pass a sub-level.
